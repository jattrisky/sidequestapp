Privacy Policy

Side Quest: Focus RPG — Last updated: February 24, 2026

Introduction

Side Quest ("we", "our", or "the app") is a focus and productivity app that gamifies task completion through an AI-generated RPG experience. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Data stored on your device:

  • Hero profile (name, archetype, appearance preferences)
  • Quest history and completion records
  • Loot, clothing, and achievement data
  • App preferences and settings
  • Focus timer statistics (duration, streaks)

Data synced via iCloud:

  • All of the above data is synced through Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) if you have iCloud enabled on your device. This sync is handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data sent to third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API: When you embark on a quest, we send your hero name, archetype, world theme, and quest context (task name, NPC names, story history) to Google's Gemini API to generate personalized narrative content, images, and voice narration. We do not send any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your real name, email, or location. Google's use of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit: Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Data we do NOT collect:

  • We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, or physical address
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not share or sell any data to third parties
  • We do not create user accounts or require login

Apple Reminders Access

Side Quest can optionally access your Apple Reminders to import tasks as quests. This access requires your explicit permission and is used solely to display your reminder titles within the app. Reminder data is not sent to any server or third party.

AI-Generated Content

Side Quest uses Google's Gemini AI to generate story narratives, character images, loot artwork, and voice narration. The prompts sent to Gemini contain only fictional game data (hero name, archetype, world setting, story context). No real-world personal data is included in these requests.

Notifications

With your permission, Side Quest sends local notifications for quest timers, streak reminders, and focus encouragement. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and are not sent through any external service.

Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If iCloud sync is enabled, data is encrypted and synced through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. We do not operate our own servers or databases.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is stored on your device for as long as the app is installed. To delete all app data, simply delete the Side Quest app from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, you can also remove synced data through Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage.

Children's Privacy

Side Quest is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
