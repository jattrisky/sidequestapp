Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchase." Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Side Quest Pro to manage or cancel.

Is my data backed up?

Yes. If iCloud is enabled on your device, your hero, quests, and loot are automatically synced across your devices via Apple's CloudKit.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Reset All Data" to erase everything. Alternatively, deleting the app removes all local data. To remove iCloud data, go to iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage.
